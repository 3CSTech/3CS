Automated Deployment

Automated deployment is the practice of automating software releases by coded scripts requiring no manual or human intervention. This leads to three main benefits:

  • Repeatable - the same script can be used over and over again
  • Reliability - you can be assured of the same result time after time
  • Quicker - release times are massively reduced from hours to minutes and sometimes seconds
